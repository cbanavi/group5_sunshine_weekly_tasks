3. FINRA
Write a method which prints out the numbers from 1 to 30 but for numbers which are a multiple of 3, print "FIN" instead of the number
and for numbers which are a multiple of 5, print "RA" instead of the numbers. For numbers which are a multiple of both 3 and 5,
print "FINRA" instead of the number.
